Briefing 5 Since the Republic of Redonia's founding in the fires of revolution, its agents have long labored to 
liberate other neighboring peoples. Now, with tension between Redonia and Ormosa building, the opportunity 
has arrived to topple Ormosa's ancient regime, and unite the two nations.
 But this will not happen quietly. Redonia is ringed by hostile nations that will look on jealously, none 
more so than the Kingdom of South Redonia, still licking its wounds after the devestation of the civil war...
